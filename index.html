Still Crazy After All These Years

Events shape attitudes and opinions. This application will focus on using basic graphs to show information on the ages of people around the US and around the world, and relate that age information to various events.

Data Sources and Extraction

Data is collected from http://www.census.gov/popest/data/state/asrh/2014/SC-EST2014-AGESEX-CIV.html and https://www.census.gov/population/international/data/idb/region.php
Once data is collected, the csv files were opened in Excel and only columns required for plotting the graphs were retained.
Also the data related to different coutries were extracted and formatted similar to what we have for US states.

Frameworks, Libraries used

We used d3js for manipulating dom elements. Used Bootstrap for styling.
