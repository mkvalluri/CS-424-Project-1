Few Observations

Below are few observations made from the information and graphs

  • For most of the states in USA, as the years are progressing, the curve is also moving till 75, but there on it stops. Probably we can say that average max age of people in US is around 70.
  • California has the highest population, followed by New York and Texas.
  • Population above 80 was decreasing in Florida
  • Greece, UAE have more number of people in the age group of 30 - 50 than that of from 0-30
  • India, USA has more population in lower age groups compared to that of higher age groups.
